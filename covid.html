Covid Information
If you have COVID-19:
Stay home until your fever is gone and avoid contact with others. If you cannot stay away from others, please wear a mask. Wash your hands regularly.
Go to an emergency department/call 911 if you:
  • Find it hard to breathe
  • Have chest pain
  • Can't drink anything
  • Feel very sick
  • Feel confused

Source: BC Centre for Disease Control
